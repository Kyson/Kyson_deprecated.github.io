如图：

![code-completion](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Kyson/Kyson.github.io/master/images/post_img/AndroidStudio%E5%88%9D%E5%A7%8B%E5%8C%96%E8%AE%BE%E7%BD%AE/code-completion.jpg)
